Born in 2026, The Broadway redefines nightlife in South Pattaya. We're not just another venue—we're a theatrical experience. Live music echoes through our intimate spaces. International DJs command the energy. Dancer shows captivate. Our Isan cuisine and premium cocktails set the stage for unforgettable nights.

Indoor & outdoor spaces blend sophistication with raw energy. Every night is a production. Every guest is part of the show.
